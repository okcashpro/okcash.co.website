Secure your wallet

  • OK
  • Secure your wallet

Just like a physical wallet, your MetaMask wallet must be secured. Okcash enables you to transfer value easily and puts you in full control of your funds. With this freedom comes the responsibility to ensure high security. Always adopt good security practices to protect your funds.

Be Cautious with Online Services

Exercise caution with online wallets and exchanges, as they are often targets for security breaches and may lack insurance and robust security for long-term storage. Ensure MetaMask is installed directly from official sources, and always enable two-factor authentication (2FA) if offered for accounts linked to exchanges where you may buy or sell OK.

Use MetaMask for Everyday Transactions, Consider Cold Storage for Savings

Think of your MetaMask wallet as you would a physical wallet: only keep small amounts for everyday transactions. For larger or long-term holdings, consider using an offline or hardware wallet to maximize security.

Backup Your MetaMask Recovery Phrase

MetaMask provides a 12-word recovery phrase when you create a new wallet. This recovery phrase is crucial to accessing your OK funds on any device. Store this phrase securely, offline, and avoid digital copies like screenshots. You can write it down and store it in a safe place (such as a vault or safe deposit box) to protect against unauthorized access.

Protect Your Recovery Phrase

This phrase is the key to your wallet. Anyone with access to it can fully control your funds, so treat it like the password to your bank account. Avoid sharing it with anyone, and never enter it on any site except within MetaMask’s official app or browser extension.

Avoid Digital Storage of Your Recovery Phrase

For maximum security, keep your recovery phrase offline. If you must store a digital copy, ensure it is encrypted and stored in a secure digital vault. However, offline storage (like paper or a metal backup) is the safest choice.

Set a Strong MetaMask Password

During the setup of MetaMask, create a strong password to add a layer of security. This password is required to unlock your wallet on your device and helps protect against unauthorized access in case someone gains access to your device.

Never Forget Your Password

Losing your MetaMask password won’t result in the loss of funds if you have your recovery phrase. However, it’s a best practice to store your password securely, as losing both the password and recovery phrase would mean permanent loss of access to your OK funds.

Use a Strong, Unique Password

Ensure your password is at least 16 characters long and includes a mix of letters, numbers, and symbols. Avoid using common words or personal information.

Use Cold Storage for Long-Term Savings

For maximum security, consider using a hardware wallet (cold storage) for larger OK holdings. This method keeps your funds offline, reducing exposure to potential online threats. Cold storage is ideal for long-term savings, while MetaMask is best suited for regular transactions.

Keep MetaMask and Browser Software Updated

Regular updates to MetaMask and your web browser ensure you receive the latest security patches and features. This is essential for reducing vulnerabilities and maximizing the protection of your funds. Enable auto-updates for MetaMask and all related software to stay secure.

Plan for Asset Transfer

To prevent the permanent loss of your OK funds in case of an emergency, consider creating a secure plan for passing on access to your recovery phrase. Keep your family or beneficiaries informed, or create a legal arrangement to ensure your recovery information is accessible when needed.
