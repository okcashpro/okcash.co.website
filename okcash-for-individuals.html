The Future of Social Payments

Okcash on mobiles is a perfect way to tip, share OK or pay for products and services on the go. It allows you to pay in a real easy and fast way by either scanning a QR code to make the payment, or by typing the address directly to send the coins. No need to sign up, swipe cards, type a PIN, or anything else. All you need to receive OK payments is to display the QR code in your Okcash app and let your friend scan your mobile. You can also use some of the Social networks to send OK anywhere without the need for the recipient to even have an Okcash wallet installed to be able to get them.

Security, coin control

Okcash transactions are secured by military grade cryptography. Nobody can charge you money or make a payment on your behalf. And as long as you take the required steps to secure your wallet, Okcash can give you full control over your money and a strong level of protection against many types of fraud. You are always in control of your own money and can use the coin control system to have even more detailed experience.

Works everywhere all the time

Just like the world wide web. The Okcash Network is live 24/7 365 days per year and accessible from any part of the world with an internet connection, it is always available and accessible by any of the different wallets or services. You are free to use either one of the Online wallets, the core wallet, the daemons or one of the different multiwallets for Android for Okcash, so every user can select their favorite clients. No problem there; All clients are compatible as they use the same open technology. The network is decentralized hence is free from middle men and central points of failure.

Fast and easy International payments

Sending OK across borders is as easy as sending an email. There are no waiting times like with banks, nor extra fees or special limitations like the minimum or maximum amount you can send, you have full control and the reciever will see the payment reflected in seconds.

Lowest fees on market

There is no fee to receive Okcash, and the fee for sending is just 0.0001 OKcash no matter where you send it, being one of the lowest fees in the market. On some wallets you can control how large a fee to pay when spending. higher fees can encourage even faster confirmations of your transactions.

Protect your identity

With Okcash, there is no data that some malicious actor can collect in order to impersonate you. In fact by using good habits to protect your privacy you can send a payment without revealing your identity.
